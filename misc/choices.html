Technology Choices

After our research, we believe that the popularization and use of robots should still be supported, because their help is huge, but we need to fully understand its function and performance before using it.

The risks of using robots can be avoided as much as possible, because robots are always controlled by humans. And robots will never replace humans, it will only help humans work and live better.
