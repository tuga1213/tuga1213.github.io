Robot Companions and Caregivers

As the global ageing problem worsens, the declining birth rate reduces the proportion of young people who can provide care, so that societies now often lack adequate care for older persons.

At the same time, the pressure of young people's lives is increasing, work occupies most of young people's time, and there is not enough energy to take care of the elderly.

At the same time, it also seriously ignores the well-being of the disabled, the inconvenience and high cost of travel, and the discrimination of others make the life of the disabled more and more difficult.
