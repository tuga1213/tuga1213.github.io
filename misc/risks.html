Risks

Robot companions are also at risk, especially for the elderly and disabled who cannot take care of themselves, and does all-round monitoring and detection violating their privacy?

Machine failure is also a problem to consider, due to power outages or system errors may cause harm to the elderly and disabled.

At the same time, humanoid robots may cause the user to feel strange or disgusted, resulting in the user's rejection of the robot companion.
